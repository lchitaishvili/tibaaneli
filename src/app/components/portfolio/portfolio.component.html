Our winery

At Tibaaneli Winery, we take pride in our vineyard, where we carefully nurture and select the finest grapes. Our vineyard's unique terroir and sustainable farming practices ensure that every grape meets our exacting standards. Our winemakers personally oversee the entire process from vine to bottle.

Our commitment to quality extends throughout our winemaking journey. We combine traditional Georgian winemaking techniques with modern technology to create wines that truly express the character of our land. Every bottle tells a story of our passion for winemaking and our dedication to producing exceptional wines that honor Georgia's rich viticultural heritage.
